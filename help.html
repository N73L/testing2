What is AiderBot?

-AIDERBOT is a chatbot designed to assist users with common medical concerns by providing general treatment advice and proven home remedies.

Can AiderBot diagnose medical conditions?

-No, AIDERBOT cannot diagnose medical conditions. It offers general information and suggestions but should not replace professional medical consultation.

Is the information provided by AiderBot reliable?

-AIDERBOT provides information based on widely recognized medical knowledge and home remedies. However, it is important to verify the information with a healthcare professional for serious concerns.

Can AiderBot help with emergency situations?

-AIDERBOT is not designed to handle medical emergencies. In case of an emergency, please contact a healthcare professional or dial emergency services immediately.

Does AiderBot provide personalized medical advice?

-AIDERBOT offers general guidance based on the symptoms you describe but does not offer personalized medical advice tailored to your specific health history.

Can I use AIDERBOT for children or elderly people?

-AIDERBOT can provide general advice for both children and elderly individuals, but it is crucial to consult a pediatrician or geriatrician for age-specific concerns.

What should I do if AIDERBOT’s suggestions don’t work or I feel worse?

-If the suggestions provided by AIDERBOT do not improve your condition or if you feel worse, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional.

How accurate are the home remedies provided by AIDERBOT?

-The home remedies provided by AIDERBOT are based on established practices, but individual responses may vary. It is always best to consult a professional before trying new treatments.

Can AIDERBOT help with mental health concerns?

-AIDERBOT can provide general advice on managing stress and anxiety but should not be used as a substitute for professional mental health support.

How can I report an issue or give feedback on AIDERBOT?

-If you experience any issues or have feedback regarding AIDERBOT, please contact our support team or use the feedback option within the platform.
